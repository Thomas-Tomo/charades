Charades is a game where players guess a word or phrase that another player is trying to act out without speaking.

The main rules are:

  • Players take turns acting out a word or phrase without speaking while the others try to guess what it is.
  • No talking or making sounds is allowed while acting.
  • Players can use hand signals to indicate the number of words or syllables in the phrase and its category (movie, book, etc.).
  • The acting player can point to specific parts of their body to represent words like "sounds like" or "rhymes with".
  • The guessing players have a limited time to guess the word or phrase correctly.
